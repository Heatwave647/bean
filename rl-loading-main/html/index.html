Mission Briefing: Scavenger Protocol

Welcome, Operator. You have crossed the Red Line. Cayo Perico is a temporal anomalyâ€”a pocket of prehistoric time violently pushed into our reality. The initial top-secret expedition failed; they were overwhelmed by a world that refuses to be tamed.

Under the Scavenger Protocol, you are deployed to this hostile island. Your purpose: retrieve vital data, secure lost assets, and survive against threats both ancient and modern. This is a fast-paced, objective-based PvPvE survival shooter.

Field Regulations

  • Zero Tolerance: Cheating, exploiting, or hacking results in a permanent ban. No exceptions.
  • Conduct: Toxicity, racism, and harassment will not be tolerated. This is an intense, competitive environment, but respect is mandatory.
  • Fair Play: Stream sniping and using third-party comms to gain an unfair advantage over players not in your squad is forbidden.
  • Combat Integrity: Disconnecting during an active firefight ("combat logging") to save your gear is a punishable offense.
  • Objective Focus: Random Deathmatch (RDM) is not the goal. Engage enemies to secure objectives, defend your position, or survive an encounter.

Survival Guide: Cayo Perico

Factions: Choose your allegiance. The disciplined Military faction seeks to contain the anomaly, while the resourceful Survivalists focus on independent extraction and profit.

The Threats:

  • Other Players (PvP): Every other squad on the island is a potential rival competing for the same objectives.
  • Rival Factions (PvE): Hostile AI representing rogue military and other scavenger teams patrol the island.
  • Prehistoric Life (PvE): Dinosaurs are a constant threat. Raptors hunt in packs and large carnivores are territorial. Use them to your advantage or become their prey.
